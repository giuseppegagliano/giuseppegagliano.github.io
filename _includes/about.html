About

Building production ML systems means owning the full picture - from experiment design through deployment, monitoring, and reliability. That's where I operate.

At HelloFresh I build and maintain the ML infrastructure that tells procurement what to buy and customers which recipes to cook. I lead the addon demand forecasting stack - PySpark, Prophet, LightGBM, Airflow on Databricks - serving inventory decisions across every international market. I co-lead recipe recommendation and cold-start ranking systems live across US, GB, FR, BE, CA, and AU. I design A/B experiments with Statsig and Optimizely, drive MLOps migrations on MLflow, and actively contribute to incident response when predictions go wrong.

Lately I've moved into a new layer: building LLM-powered developer tooling with LangGraph and the Claude API that makes the whole team faster.
